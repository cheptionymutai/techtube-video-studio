## Your Three-Part Success Engine: Share, Optimize, Promote

Think of the video we’ve delivered as a key to a brand-new, fully-fueled race car. You can’t win the race by just sitting in the driver's seat and admiring the dashboard. You have to start the engine, map your route, and put your foot on the accelerator.

### 1. SHARE Relentlessly (The Free, High-Impact Work)
Before you even think about spending a shilling on ads, you must exhaust every "free" channel you own. "Posting it once" is not a strategy; it's a whisper. You need to shout.

* **Embed it in your email signature:** Every email your team sends is now a potential video view.
* **Give it to your sales team:** This is the most powerful move. Train your team to send the video to new prospects before a meeting to warm them up. It’s their 24/7 sales assistant.
* **Pin it to your profiles:** Pin the video to the top of your LinkedIn, Facebook, and X (Twitter) profiles.
* **Use it in your newsletter:** Make it the feature story in your next email blast to your existing customers.
* **Onboard new hires:** Use your brand story video to get new employees excited and aligned with your mission from day one.

### 2. OPTIMIZE Intelligently (The Smart Work)
The 3-minute brand story we created for your website is not the right video for an Instagram Reel. One size does not fit all in the digital world.

* **Format for the platform:** A video on LinkedIn should be a square (1:1 ratio). A video for Instagram Stories or TikTok must be vertical (9:16).
* **Hook them fast:** The first 3 seconds are all that matter on a social feed. The optimized version of your video might need to start with a powerful quote, not a slow-fade intro.
* **Captions are non-negotiable:** Over 80% of video on social media is watched with the sound OFF. An un-captioned video is a silent movie that no one understands.

*This is where working with a scalable video partner truly pays off. Our subscription models are designed to give you all the optimized cut-downs you need to succeed on every platform.*

### 3. PROMOTE Strategically (The Growth Work)
This is the accelerator. Relying on organic reach (just your existing followers) is like driving your race car in first gear. Paid promotion is how you hit fifth gear.

* **Target your ideal customer:** Don’t just "boost post." Use ad tools on LinkedIn and Facebook to put your video in front of people by job title, company size, or location (like specific neighborhoods in Nairobi or all of East Africa).
* **Amplify what works:** After running a small ad test, you’ll see what message resonates. You can then put a larger budget behind the winning video, confident that your money is generating real leads.
