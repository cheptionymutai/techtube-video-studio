If you prefer to read, here is the simple workflow to get your strategy today:

First, visit [techtubestudio.com](https://techtubestudio.com) and click **"Build My Strategy Now."** StratBot will ask about your industry and your website link. 

#### 2. The AI Scan
Once you provide your URL, StratBot scans your site to understand exactly what your business does. In the video, we used the *Kenya Revenue Authority* as an example, and the AI accurately summarized their core services in seconds.

#### 3. Define Your Goals
You get to choose the parameters that fit your budget and goals:
* **Duration:** Plan for 1 to 6 months.

* **Platforms:** Select where you’ll be sharing (LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, etc.).

#### 4. Instant Results & Pricing
This is the best part. StratBot doesn't just give you "ideas"—it presents a **detailed quarterly strategy** along with an estimated monthly cost for us to implement it for you.
